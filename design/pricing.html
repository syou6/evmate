Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EV Mate Japan and your data privacy.

Is my Tesla account data safe? expand_more
Absolutely. We use official Tesla API tokens and never store your raw password. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We prioritize your privacy and security above all else.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time? expand_more
Yes, you can cancel your subscription instantly from your account settings. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
Does using EV Mate drain my battery? expand_more
We've optimized our polling frequency to minimize phantom drain. By default, we let your car sleep when it's idle and only wake it up during expected activity or charging sessions.
What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We use Stripe for payment processing, which accepts all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and Apple Pay / Google Pay.
